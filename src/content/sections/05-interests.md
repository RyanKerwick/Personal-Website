  <span class="label">Strength training</span>
  <h3>The <em>gym</em></h3>
  <p>
    I lift four days a week on a linear progression that refuses to be rushed. The gym is
    where I practice the slow kind of patience that also makes debugging a flaky I²C bus
    bearable. Same principle: small, repeatable inputs, tracked honestly.
  </p>

  <span class="label">Embedded tinkering</span>
  <h3>The <em>workbench</em></h3>
  <p>
    Somewhere between a hobby and a discipline. I collect microcontrollers, wire up things
    that don't strictly need wiring up, and occasionally produce a small tool that's
    genuinely useful. If it blinks or beeps on command, I consider it a good weekend.
